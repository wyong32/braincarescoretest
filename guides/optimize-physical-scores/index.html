How to Optimize Your Physical Brain Care Score

The Physical category of the McCance Brain Care Score includes blood pressure, blood sugar, cholesterol, and body mass index. These are medical measurements, so the safest first step is to review recent readings with a qualified clinician.

Blood pressure

Bring home readings or recent clinic readings to your appointment. Ask your clinician what range is appropriate for your age, medical history, and medication plan.

Blood sugar and cholesterol

A1c and cholesterol usually require lab testing. If your values are outside the worksheet's higher-score ranges, discuss nutrition, activity, medication, and follow-up timing with your health care provider.

BMI

BMI is a screening measure, not a complete picture of health. Use it as one conversation starter alongside waist circumference, strength, mobility, nutrition, and medical history.

Score basics

What is a Good Brain Care Score?

The McCance Brain Care Score ranges from 0 to 21 points. In the official framework, a higher score represents better current brain care across physical, lifestyle, and social-emotional factors.

Is there one universal good score?

The most useful goal is not a single magic number. Mass General emphasizes reviewing the score with a health care provider and improving it over time at a sustainable pace.

How to think about the maximum score

A 21 out of 21 means the worksheet's highest-point option was selected in every domain. Many people will have room to improve in one or more areas, especially measurements that require clinical support such as blood pressure, A1c, cholesterol, or sleep disorders.

What to do next

Calculate your current score, print it, and bring it to a primary care visit. Use the category breakdown to ask specific questions rather than trying to overhaul everything at once.

How to Improve Your Brain Care Score

The safest way to improve your Brain Care Score is to work category by category and make sustainable changes. Discuss medical measurements and treatment questions with a qualified health professional.

Physical

Blood pressure, blood sugar, cholesterol, and BMI are best reviewed with a clinician. Bring recent lab values and home readings to your appointment so your provider can help you interpret them.

Lifestyle

The worksheet includes nutrition, alcohol, smoking, aerobic activity, and sleep. Practical starting points include more fruits and vegetables, whole grains, lean protein, less sodium, fewer sugar-sweetened beverages, regular walking, and a consistent sleep routine.

Social Emotional

Stress, social relationships, and meaning in life are part of the score. Consider stress-management practices, reaching out to trusted people, joining community activities, and seeking professional support when stress or mood makes daily life difficult.

Make it measurable

Retake the score periodically and compare category totals. Small improvements can be easier to sustain than drastic changes.

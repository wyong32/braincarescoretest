Score interpretation

What Does a Brain Care Score of 18 Mean?

A Brain Care Score of 18 out of 21 is near the high end of the McCance Brain Care Score scale. It means you selected high-point answers in many domains, but there are still up to 3 points that may be available depending on your physical, lifestyle, or social-emotional profile.

Do not treat 18 as a diagnosis

The score is an educational care tracker, not a diagnostic test. It should not be used to start, stop, or change medication without professional guidance.

Where the missing points matter

An 18 can mean different things depending on which category lost points. Missing points in blood pressure, A1c, cholesterol, or BMI should be discussed with a clinician. Missing points in lifestyle or social-emotional areas may suggest practical habits to review over time.

Best next step

Print your result, note which domains were below the maximum, and ask your primary care physician which changes are appropriate for your medical history.

5 Lifestyle Changes for a Healthier Brain

The Lifestyle category of the Brain Care Score focuses on nutrition, alcohol, smoking, aerobic activity, and sleep. Small, sustainable changes are usually easier to maintain than abrupt overhauls.

1. Improve nutrition quality

Build meals around fruits and vegetables, lean protein, whole grains, less sodium, and fewer sugar-sweetened beverages.

2. Review alcohol use

If you drink, compare your weekly pattern with the worksheet and ask your clinician what is safe for your medications and health history.

3. Get support for smoking cessation

If you currently smoke, evidence-based support can make quitting more realistic. Ask your clinician about counseling, medication, or local programs.

4. Add regular aerobic activity

Walking counts as moderate activity. Start with a level that is safe for you and build gradually.

5. Protect sleep

Sleep duration and untreated sleep disorders both matter in the worksheet. Discuss persistent snoring, insomnia, or daytime sleepiness with a professional.
